FAQ

  • How are your tours priced? Tours are priced at $25 per hour and you can decide how many hours you would like to spend with the student and what you would like to do. For example, the tour could just consist of a walking tour but it could also include eating in the dining hall, attending a class, going to a game, talking to a professor, and seeing a dorm room.
  • Can I shadow a student and attend a class? Yes! The “college tours” section of the website includes all the majors/minors/certificates of the student tour guides. If you find someone with the same major, email them and ask what classes they are taking. Then, find a time to shadow one of their classes.
  • Can a prospective high school student attend alone? Yes! Tours can be given to just high school students, parents, or families. However, for attending a class and talking to a professor, only the student can participate.
