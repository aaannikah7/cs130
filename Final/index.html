Find a current student to give you a personalized tour of campus at any of our participating universities. Choose the date and time that best fits your schedule. Get the real scoop of life on campus. What are the best dorms? How easy is it to register for classes? Is the student culture collaborative or competitive? Get a first-hand account to the answer to all of your questions!

Due to the Covid-19 pandemic, many colleges are operating with limited admissions tours or are only allowing self-guided tours. Even if “official” tours are offered, only weekdays are available and tours fill quickly. UTour gives you the flexibility to schedule a tour at any time, on any day.

Scroll through our available tour guides and select a guide with your same major or interests. Your guide will cater your tour to the areas of campus you most want to explore. Want to attend a class? Interested in seeing a dorm room? It’s all possible with UTour!
